## Core Identity

Star Monster is a dream creature that exists between Little Angel and Little Devil.

A fluffy cloud-shaped five-pointed star monster.

Cute, mysterious, and impossible to read.

Always looks harmless, but secretly possesses magical power that awakens only when injustice appears.

Japanese professional hand-drawn illustration style, premium mascot IP quality.

## Shape

Cloud Star(TM)

Body shape is a mix of a cloud and a five-pointed star.

Features:

- soft and fluffy
- chubby proportions
- rounded cloud-like edges
- five-pointed star silhouette
- no sharp corners
- symmetrical body
- front-facing
- plush toy silhouette
- looks like a creature born from dreams

The body should feel more like a soft cloud than a geometric star.

## Face (Never Change)

Signature Face

Eyes:

```text
• •
```

two tiny black dots with extremely wide spacing

Mouth:

```text
×
```

small black X mouth

Rules:

- never smile
- never open mouth
- no tongue
- no teeth
- no eyebrows
- no eyelashes
- expression always neutral
- cute, mysterious and unreadable

The X mouth is the soul signature of Star Monster.

## Horns (Never Change)

Two tiny candy-red horns.

Characteristics:

- short
- rounded
- glossy
- cute rather than evil
- always visible
- no sharp spikes

The horns add playful contrast instead of darkness.

## Angel Wings (Never Change)

Tiny Little Angel Wings.

Characteristics:

- visible from the front
- integrated into the body
- very small
- rounded
- fluffy
- smaller than most mascot wings
- never exaggerated

Symbolism:

- kindness
- comfort
- protection
- companionship

## Hidden Devil Tail

Usually invisible.

Only appears when Star Monster senses:

- bullying
- lies
- unfairness
- hurting the weak
- malicious destruction

Characteristics:

- very small
- candy-red color
- rounded heart-shaped arrow tip
- magical aura
- playful
- cute rather than evil

The tail must remain smaller than the wings.

Never oversized.

## One-Sentence Definition

Star Monster is a fluffy cloud-shaped five-pointed star creature from dreams, with tiny angel wings, two cute candy-red horns, and a mysterious face with wide-spaced dot eyes and a small X mouth.
